Get to know me

Hi, I'm Mehrin Firdousi, and I'm a Computer Science student. I was born and brought up in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. As far as academics are concerned, nothing keeps me as engaged as coding and problem-solving, solely because of the immeasurable possibilities I come across every time. I see it as something more than just my major, and although I've barely scratched the surface yet, I aspire to become proficient at creating solutions to real-life problems through my code and creativity. Besides CS, my core interests and skills are centered around art, photography, music, and writing. I personally consider pursuing these activities just as important as performing well in my CS courses, as I believe these are the things that truly make me who I am.

HOMELAND

Where I'm from

I am from Kerala, India. Travelling back home every year is definitely something I look forward to, that I've missed this year due to COVID-19. I find the environment there therapeutic in a way; it's a bit of an escape from all the bustle we have here in the UAE. My father is from Thrissur, while my mom is from Ernakulam, both of which are special homes to me.
